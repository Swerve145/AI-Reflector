The extension seamlessly integrates into your ChatGPT workflow, adding a simple but powerful reflection step:

1

How to Write Quality Reflections

‚úÖ
DO: Be Specific
Instead of "This helped", explain exactly what you learned and why it makes sense
‚úÖ
DO: Make Connections
Link new information to concepts you already understand
‚úÖ
DO: Ask Questions
Note what you're still confused about or want to explore further
‚ùå
DON'T: Copy Text

‚ùå
DON'T: Be Generic
Responses like "Good answer" don't help your learning
‚ùå
DON'T: Rush
